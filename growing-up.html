I grew up in a the heart of the central in the town of Madera, although I was born only a stone-throws away in the city of Fresno. Madera is a growing community that is at the foothills of the Sierras near Yosemite. It is a small and booming town with friendly people (and a Mexican Restaurant to found in most major cross-streets). I also teach here and use my story to help those that are young and wanting to see opportunities are out there for them.
